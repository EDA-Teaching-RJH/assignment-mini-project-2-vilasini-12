Method:
1. Pat the tofu dry with kitchen paper and cut into 20–25 cubes. Preheat the air fryer to 200C.
2. Mix the soy sauce, oil, garlic, ginger, paprika and salt together in a bowl. Stir the tofu cubes into the soy mixture and then sprinkle in the cornflour, stirring gently until evenly coated.
3. Air fry for 12–15 minutes, shaking halfway through, until crisp and golden. Serve immediately with the chilli sauce for dipping.
